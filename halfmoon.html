Khan MNM Sadh
I study theory and applications of computer science and work as a software engineer for Google. I also write, make art and build things.

I’m interested in linguistics, logic and epistemology. I’ve recently been focusing on computational linguistics and AI, specifically applications of natural language understanding.

I’ve worked in a wide area of software engineering including backend, infrastructure, systems, web, apps and have built cool stuff for Google, Samsung and TCI. Outside of work, I’m involved in some projects which are open sourced on GitHub.

I post blogs on inside the insight. I share my Bengali writings on Sadhubôchôn and Sulol Songroho. I am also involved as editor and publisher at Bengali literary community Lekhalikhi. I love to play with colors, draw pictures and make graphic artworks; you can browse them on my arts portfolio. I use a Bengali keyboard layout named Sonnivo which happened to be designed by me. I’m also an amateur vexillophile and I’ll gladly welcome fellow contributors on Flags.fyi.

I was born and raised in Bangladesh, and have studied computer science at KUET and Stony Brook. I've dwelled in Dhaka, New York and now live in Mountain View, California.
